ABOUT IIFOMAS

IIFOMAS (India International Forum on Mine Action and Safety)

India International Forum on Mine Action and Safety (IIFOMAS), is an independent Indian think tank on Global Mine Action Scenario and Demilitarization of obsolete and abandoned weapons and explosive devices (DEMIL). As India’s premier think tank for mine action, IIFOMAS unites experts, government bodies, and international organizations to strategize, innovate, and implement sustainable solutions for mine clearance, community safety, and humanitarian aid.

Knowledge gained by IIFOMAS will assist in strategizing Mine Action activities across the Globe. IIFOMAS is an initiative of the Horizon Safer World Foundation (HSWF) and is driven by a team of dedicated Promoters with over two decades of experience in every aspect of Mine Action.

Our Vision

A world where communities are empowered to rebuild and thrive in safe environments, free from the threat of landmines and explosive hazards, through collaborative and sustainable Mine Action efforts.

Our Mission

To drive a unified global effort in Mine Action by advancing innovative solutions to mitigate explosive hazards and empowering communities to rebuild and thrive in safe, resilient environments worldwide through strategic alliances, pioneering research, and progressive policy development
